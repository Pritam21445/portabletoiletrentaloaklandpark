Frequently Asked Questions

Can you really help me book other event rentals?

Yes. We have a network of trusted local partners. While we can't guarantee availability, we can often bundle services like tents, tables, and fencing to simplify your planning process.

What's the best option for a fundraiser or charity event?

To show donors you value them, we recommend our Deluxe Flushing units. They offer a significant upgrade in comfort over a standard unit for a very modest increase in the porta potty rental price.

I'm planning a pop-up. What do I need to know about your trailers?

Our restroom rental trailer units require a flat, level surface and access to reliable power and water. We need to coordinate delivery with your other vendors. Call us early in your planning process.

What does a luxury restroom trailer cost?

The fancy porta potty rental cost depends on the size of the trailer and the duration of your event. It's a premium partnership for clients who require the best. Contact us for a customized quote.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

We always do our best to work with the budgets of registered non-profit organizations. Please call us to discuss your event and your needs.
